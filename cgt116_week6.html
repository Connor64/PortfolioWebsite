Wk6 Character Blockout - Club Penguin by connor64 on Sketchfab

For this assignment, I decided to model the penguin from Club Penguin. I downloaded an image from the game that was used in the player's menu as a reference image and (crudely) drew my own side profile. I put these in as free image panes and used a unit cone model as my base for the body. All other apendages of the penguin are separate meshes.
